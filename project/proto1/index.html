How do you
visualize music?

About...

Certain colors are associated with certain moods and have the power to affect the emotional and mental state of people. Music is also a common source of relaxation for people that feel a need to destress and relieve themselves of anxiety or negative emotions. These findings bring forth the question of how music and color can work together to create a soothing yet effective and powerful way for people to connect with their emotions in a way that brings forth visual creativity and accesses personal music preferences.

Instructions

  1. Upload your audiofile (mp3).
  2. Select one of the three choices for the type of audiovisualizer that will be displayed
  3. Tap the drop-down menu (located between the audio upload button and the audio control buttons) to display the color picker menu.
    *To make the menu disappear, just tap the menu again!*
