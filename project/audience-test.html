Target Audience Test

Tasks

  1. Customize their own music player.
  2. Navigate easily through the different buttons and menus/options for the music player.
  3. Navigate easily between different sections/pages.

Questions

  1. How easy was it for you to navigate through the application?
  2. Did you find it was easy to get where you wanted to on the application?
  3. How clear were instructions?
  4. How did the application affect you mentally and emotionally?
  5. Were you prompted to think about music in a new way after using the application?
